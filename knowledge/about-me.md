## Bench Variant Definitions

Sidney tracks three bench standards:

1. **IPF 2026 (comp standard):** Pause/motionless on chest, commands, butt on bench, no heave/bounce.
2. **Normal gym, no wraps:** Touch-and-go with light to moderate bounce, no commands, butt stays on bench but less strictly than comp.
3. **Gym + wraps:** Butt may lift, moderate bounce, wrist wraps + elbow wraps for max 1RM boost.

Most logged data is standard 2 (normal gym, no wraps) unless noted.
